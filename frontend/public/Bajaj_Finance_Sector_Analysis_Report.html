Bajaj Finance Sector Analysis

Date: December 10, 2024

Source: ICICI Securities Equity Research Report

Venture Capital (VC) Perspective

While Bajaj Finance is a mature financial services player, its innovation-driven transformation under BFL 3.0—especially its AI-first strategy—makes it a valuable reference point for venture capitalists looking to invest in fintech, embedded finance, and AI-led customer engagement startups.

  • Adoption of 29 GenAI use cases across 25 workstreams targeting INR 1.5bn in savings by FY26.
  • App installs target: 150-170mn by FY29 from 61.7mn (FY24); web visitors projected to 4,500mn by FY29.
  • Targeting 25% PAT CAGR with expanding AUM and cross-sell franchise.

Investment Banking (IB) Perspective

Bajaj Finance offers an excellent case for coverage through M&A advisory, capital markets transactions, or debt issuance given its strong capital structure, market positioning, and technological transformation roadmap.

  • Target Price: INR 8,500 based on SoTP (Standalone: INR 7,050, Housing Subsidiaries: INR 1,450).
  • RoE targets of 20-22% and strong EPS growth from INR 170.4 (FY23) to projected INR 305.3 (FY26).
  • Comprehensive financials and key ratios available through FY26 for accurate DCF or relative valuation modeling.

Private Equity (PE) Perspective

With a clear growth roadmap, AI-led transformation, and detailed segmental strategy, Bajaj Finance provides excellent benchmarks or a model for PE deals in NBFCs or AI-led financial services firms.

  • 3.2-3.5% credit market share by FY29, 190-210mn customer franchise target.
  • AI-led operational efficiencies poised to enhance unit economics significantly: PAT per cross-sell franchise to grow from INR 1,374 to INR 3,800-4,000 by FY29.
  • Detailed SoTP and subsidiary valuation for segmental deal modeling and benchmarking.

Source: ICICI Securities Equity Research Report on Bajaj Finance, December 10, 2024.
